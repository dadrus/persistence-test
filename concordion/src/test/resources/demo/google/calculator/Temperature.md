# Temperature

Google can convert temperatures. Celcius to Farenheit or to Kelvin and vice versa

## [Examples](- "examples")
* [10 C in F][search] = [50][check].
* [283,15 K in C][search] = [10][check].
* [50 F in K][search] = [283,15][check].
